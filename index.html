Step into a world above the clouds

Grab your hiking boots and let's hit the trail!

At Mountain Trail Tours we want everyone to experience the wonders of the Rocky Mountains and we invite you to join us while we explore all that Colorado's mountains have to offer. We have hiking boots, backpacking packs, water bladders, waterproof clothing and so much more available for rent, as well as tour guides to help you through everything before, during, and after your trip. Happy trails!

Choose your adventure

Does the prospect of hiking 14,000ft mountains make you woozy? Not to worry! There are plenty of trails that don't require expert-level hiking experience. There are also activities that allow you to be in the mountains with minimal walking. Taking the ski lift in Telluride anyone?

Looking to go off-grid for awhile and spend a few nights out exploring? Then sign-up for our guided backpacking tours and camp by alpine lakes and see incredible wildlife--from a distance, of course.
